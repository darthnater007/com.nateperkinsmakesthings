Why Write?

Writing is my first love. I graduated from Ohio University in December of 2015 with a Bachelor of Specialized Studies Degree in Narrative Structure for Fiction, Film, Theater, Poetry, and Non-Fiction. While I write across a broad set of genres and styles, my bread and butter is crazy, off-the-wall, dark, comedic sci-fi. I was most recently published by The Cincinnati D.I.Y. Writers Group in their anthology of short fiction called The Cincinnati Corpatria: Tales From the Future. One of the short pieces I wrote for this anthology is available below for free! Current projects include workshopping old short stories for publication and working on a larger and as of yet unnamed project the involves a collection of three novellas centered around a theme.

Recent Projects

Flip

Set hundreds of years in the future after a corporate takeover of world governments by business people calling themselves The Corpatria, Ed Jones, a private eye living below the massive domes that hold the rich Topside, begins to investigate a new drug called Flip that's worming it's way up through the sewers. Published in September 2018 as part of the anthology The Cincinnati Corpatria: Tales From the Future.
